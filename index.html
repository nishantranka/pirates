How to Play

⛵ Goal & Controls

You are always the PINK ship, marked with a red ▼ — and every battle opens with a 3-second pause so you can find yourself. Your ship always sails forward — you only steer: ← → or A D. Fire with Space. Submarines dive with ↓ or S (hold). M mutes sound. Sail off one edge of the map and you emerge seamlessly from the opposite side.

🌬️ Wind

The compass (top-left) shows the wind. Your speed depends on your angle to it: sailing across the wind is fastest (~100%+), straight downwind a bit slower, and beating into the wind crawls at 40%. The wind drifts during battle and affects everyone equally — except submarines, which run on engines and ignore it.

💥 Combat

Cannons fire a broadside from whichever side faces the nearest enemy — shots fly perpendicular to your heading, and the small gun barrels on your hull show which side fires next. Reload is 1.4s for everyone, bots included. Damage falls off with distance: point-blank hits hurt ~2.5× more than max-range grazes.

🏝️ Islands

Blocky islands are deadly: the translucent blue ring is shallow water (safe), but touching the sand sinks you instantly. Islands also block cannonballs, so use them as cover — bait pursuers through tight gaps.

🚤 Ramming

Drive your bow into an enemy's side or stern to spike them: they take 3 hull damage and you take 1 in return. Meet bow-to-bow (the whole curved front counts as the bow) and both ships take 3. Glancing side-on scrapes deal nothing — they just shove the hulls apart.

🎁 Power-ups

Labeled bounties bob in the water, often just off a lethal shore (risk vs reward). Surface ships and surfaced subs can grab them:

  • ➕ +1 Health — heals one point (spawns often)
  • ⛨ Shield ×5 — absorbs the next 5 hits
  • ⚡ 2× Speed — double speed for 8s
  • ⇄ Dbl Broadside — yours to keep for 10s: fire both sides at once, with gun barrels showing on both sides (subs: torpedo spread)
  • ⁘ Rapid Fire — your next shot opens 5s of continuous fire

🤿 Submarine

Available in practice and multiplayer: engine-powered (ignores wind), fires a single bow torpedo that runs across the entire map and hits for 2× a cannonball with no distance falloff. Hold ↓/S to dive: while under you're invisible and immune to shots and rams — and you can still fire torpedoes, surfaced or submerged — but you can't grab pickups, and your dive charge (cyan bar) drains — it refills on the surface. Islands still sink you, and the maelstrom still pulls you.

🌀 Maelstrom

After a short calm, a whirlpool forms at the arena's center: an inward current tows every ship toward the middle (stronger outside the shrinking calm eye), so the fight always converges. There's no damage — but you can't out-sail it at full strength.

🏆 Scoring & Battle Modes

The host picks the win condition in the lobby. Leaderboard: score = sinking shots (8 pts, weighted most) + damage dealt (1 pt per hull point) + time survived (0.1 pt/s). Survivor: kills don't matter — only time afloat counts, and the last one standing wins (bots play cautiously and evade instead of hunting). Either way the round ends when one ship remains or every human is dead. Click the 🏆 pill (top-right) for live standings.

🤖 Multiplayer & Bots

Create a room and share the 5-letter code — or tap 🔗 Copy invite link and friends just click it to join. Or hit Fill with Bots for an instant 10-captain brawl — then +10 Bots if you want a bigger fleet (up to 21 captains). Everyone spawns scattered under a 2s golden spawn shield. If you sink, the round ends — you never spectate bots.
