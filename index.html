How to Play

⛵ Goal & Controls

Your ship always sails forward — you only steer: ← → or A D. Fire with Space. Submarines dive with ↓ or S (hold). M mutes sound. In multiplayer the last captain standing — by score — rules the seas.

🌬️ Wind

The compass (top-left) shows the wind. Your speed depends on your angle to it: sailing across the wind is fastest (~100%+), straight downwind a bit slower, and beating into the wind crawls at 40%. The wind drifts during battle and affects everyone equally — except submarines, which run on engines and ignore it.

💥 Combat

Cannons fire a broadside from whichever side faces the nearest enemy — shots fly perpendicular to your heading, so maneuver to bring your guns to bear. Reload is 1.4s for everyone, bots included. Damage falls off with distance: point-blank hits hurt ~2.5× more than max-range grazes.

🏝️ Islands

Blocky islands are deadly: the translucent blue ring is shallow water (safe), but touching the sand sinks you instantly. Islands also block cannonballs, so use them as cover — bait pursuers through tight gaps.

🚤 Ramming

Drive your bow into an enemy's flank to spike them: damage scales with your speed, how bow-on the hit is, and your hull size. Glancing scrapes just shove ships apart. The rammer takes only ~10% back — a boosted or Large ship is a battering ram.

🎁 Power-ups

Labeled bounties bob in the water, often just off a lethal shore (risk vs reward). Surface ships and surfaced subs can grab them:

  • ➕ +1 Health — heals one point (spawns often)
  • ⛨ Shield ×5 — absorbs the next 5 hits
  • ⚡ 2× Speed — double speed for 8s
  • ⇄ Dbl Broadside — fire both sides at once for 12s (subs: torpedo spread)
  • ⁘ Rapid Fire — your next shot opens 5s of continuous fire

🤿 Submarine

A multiplayer-only boat: engine-powered (ignores wind), fires a single bow torpedo — faster, longer-ranged and harder-hitting than a cannonball. Hold ↓/S to dive: while under you're invisible and immune to shots and rams, but you can't fire or grab pickups, and your dive charge (cyan bar) drains — it refills on the surface. Islands still sink you, and the maelstrom still pulls you.

🌀 Maelstrom

After a short calm, a whirlpool forms at the arena's center: an inward current tows every ship toward the middle (stronger outside the shrinking calm eye), so the fight always converges. There's no damage — but you can't out-sail it at full strength.

🏆 Scoring

Your score = sinking shots (8 pts, weighted most) + damage dealt (1 pt per hull point) + time survived (0.1 pt/s, weighted least). The round ends when one ship remains or every human is dead — the top score wins. Click the 🏆 pill (top-right) for live standings.

🤖 Multiplayer & Bots

Create a room and share the 5-letter code, or hit Fill with Bots for an instant 20-bot brawl (up to 21 captains). Everyone spawns scattered under a 2s golden spawn shield. If you sink, the round ends — you never spectate bots.
